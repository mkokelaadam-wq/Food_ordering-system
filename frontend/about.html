About FoodExpress

Delivering happiness, one meal at a time. Since 2024.

Our Story

FoodExpress was founded in 2024 with a simple mission: to make food delivery faster, easier, and more reliable. What started as a small team of 5 people has grown into a platform serving thousands of customers daily across Dar es Salaam.

We partner with the best local restaurants to bring you a wide variety of cuisines, from traditional Tanzanian favorites to international dishes. Our commitment to quality, speed, and customer satisfaction has made us one of the most trusted food delivery services in the region.
