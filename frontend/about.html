About FoodExpress

Delivering happiness, one meal at a time. Since 2024.

Our Story

FoodExpress was founded in 2024 with a simple mission: to make food delivery faster, easier, and more reliable. What started as a small team of 5 passionate food lovers has grown into a platform serving thousands of customers daily across Dar es Salaam.

We partner with the best local restaurants to bring you a wide variety of cuisines, from traditional Tanzanian favorites like Chips Mayai and Nyama Choma to international dishes. Our commitment to quality, speed, and customer satisfaction has made us one of the most trusted food delivery services in the region.

Today, we're proud to have delivered over 50,000 meals and counting. But we're just getting started. Our goal is to bring FoodExpress to every corner of Tanzania.

Our Mission

To connect people with the food they love, delivered fast and fresh. We strive to support local restaurants and create job opportunities while providing exceptional service to our customers.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's most loved food delivery platform, known for reliability, speed, and excellent customer experience. We envision a future where anyone can get their favorite meal delivered in under 30 minutes.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Fast Delivery

We guarantee hot, fresh food delivered in 45 minutes or less. Our riders are trained to be quick and careful with every order.

Quality Food

We partner only with restaurants that meet our high standards for taste, hygiene, and service. Every meal is checked before delivery.

Customer First

Your satisfaction is our priority. We listen to feedback and continuously improve to serve you better.

Support Local

We're proud to work with local restaurants and help them grow. When you order with us, you're supporting Tanzanian businesses.

Safety & Hygiene

All our partners follow strict food safety guidelines. Contactless delivery is available for your peace of mind.

24/7 Support

Our customer service team is always here to help, whether it's a question about your order or feedback about your experience.
